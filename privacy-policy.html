Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 14, 2026

At HTMLCSSJSEditor, reachable from https://htmleditor.analyticsdrive.tech/, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by HTMLCSSJSEditor and how we use it.

No Data Collection

We pride ourselves on being a zero-data-collection tool. We do not require registration, we do not have a user database, and we do not store the code you write on our servers.

Local Storage

HTMLCSSJSEditor uses browser "Local Storage" to save your current work locally on your own device. This ensures that your project is not lost if the page is refreshed or closed. This data remains on your machine and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

Log Files

HTMLCSSJSEditor follows a standard procedure of using log files by the hosting provider (GitHub Pages). These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected by log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. These are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies for marketing or user profiling. The basic operation of the site does not require cookies.

Third Party Privacy Policies

Our tool uses third-party libraries (like CodeMirror) and fonts (Google Fonts) delivered via CDN. These providers may collect your IP address as part of the asset delivery process. We recommend you consult the respective Privacy Policies of these third-party services for more detailed information.

Consent
